We are pleased to announce the upcoming release of v1.3.0, which includes significant improvements, security updates, bug fixes, and enhancements to the user interface. As part of this update, a **database migration** will be performed. During this time, **all organizations will be placed in read-only mode**.

## Scheduled Maintenance

To ensure a seamless rollout of v1.3.0, The House App will require **scheduled maintenance**. During this time, the website may be temporarily limited.

- **Date:** July 11, 2026
- **Expected Duration:** Starting at 9:00am; expected to continue until mid-afternoon
- **Impact:** Users may experience temporary interruptions in service, and all organizations will be read-only

We appreciate your understanding and patience as we implement these updates. Following the maintenance, all users will have access to the enhanced functionality and improvements provided in v1.3.0.

For a detailed overview of the changes and new features, please refer to the full release notes after the update.
